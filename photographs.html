How did I get into Photography?

For my whole life, I wanted to be a musician but, that changed Junior year of high school when we got a new band director. Band wasn't fun anymore and I wanted to try something different. I always liked taking photographs of my friends, and we always did random photoshoots. I took a photography class the next semester and fell in love with it. My parents bought me a camera and accessories and now it is my major and I have my own business. It's called Harmony Studios for anyone wondering.

My photography consists of:

  • Nature
  • Family
  • Birthdays
  • Dances
  • Sports
  • Couples
  • Graduation
  • Engagements/Weddings
